About Me

I am a dedicated Social Studies teacher, educational technology researcher, and innovator based in Gyeongsangnam-do, South Korea.

With a decade of teaching experience spanning public high schools—including Changwon Girls' High School (present), Changwon Yongho High School, and Jinhae Yongwon High School—I work at the unique intersection of humanistic social studies and digital innovation.

My mission is to empower both students and fellow educators by deploying automated, data-backed systems that transform modern school administration and classroom learning. Rather than accepting technological boundaries, I focus on building open-source systems that improve teacher agency and student growth.

The "Vibe Coding" Philosophy & Ecosystem

I firmly believe that in the age of AI, the ultimate programming language is human intent.

As an active practitioner of "Vibe Coding", I leverage natural language, precise prompt engineering, and agentic workflows to rapidly build and deploy production-ready systems.

Rather than spending hours debugging syntax, I focus on system architecture, pedagogical logic, and solving real-world classroom challenges. AI is my collaborator; it handles the code syntax while I handle the educational strategy and logic.

"The role of the educator is to teach students how to think, not how to type code. Vibe Coding shifts our energy from compiling syntax to orchestrating systems."
